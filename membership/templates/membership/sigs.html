English for Special Purposes

  • Focuses on teaching English tailored to specific disciplines or professions.
  • Emphasizes language skills relevant to learners
  • Examples include English for Business, English for Medicine, etc.

Literature

  • Explores literary texts in English, including novels, poems, plays, etc.
  • Analyzes themes, characters, literary devices, and cultural contexts.
  • Encourages critical thinking and interpretation skills.

Learning Technologies

  • Integrates technology into language teaching and learning.
  • Utilizes tools like educational software, online platforms, and multimedia resources.
  • Enhances engagement, interactivity, and accessibility in language education.

Inclusive Practice and Special Edudation Needs

  • Addresses strategies for accommodating diverse learners, including those with disabilities or special educational needs (SEN).
  • Promotes inclusive teaching methodologies and supportive learning environments.
